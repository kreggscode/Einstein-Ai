1. Introduction

Welcome to Einstein AI ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Android application.

2. Information We Collect

Einstein AI is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary for the app's functionality:

  • App Usage Data: Basic analytics to improve app performance
  • User Preferences: Settings like theme preferences, notification settings
  • Favorite Quotes: Locally stored quotes you save as favorites
  • Chat History: AI chat conversations (stored locally on your device)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • Improve user experience and app performance
  • Store your personal preferences and favorites
  • Enable AI chat features

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using:

  • Room Database: For quotes and user data
  • DataStore: For app preferences
  • Local Storage: For chat history and favorites

We do not transmit or store your data on external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

The app may use:

  • AI Services: For chat functionality (data processed securely)
  • Analytics: Optional, anonymous usage statistics

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Delete your data at any time
  • Opt-out of analytics
  • Control notification preferences

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes.
